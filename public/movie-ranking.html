The Best Movies According to Israfil

My top 3 movies of all-time.

John Wick 2

This is my favourite movie, i usually hate action movies, but this represents a sub-genre called Gun-Fu. I like the second part most because of the story and the unexpected twist at the end of the film.

Blade Runner (1983)

This is a really cool sci-fi movie. I have seen it last year, but I played the computer game back then in the 90s. The cyberpunk-like setting, awesome. The movie also awesome. The remake is not that good, also to long I guess.

Matrix

Again, a sci-fi movie, I like the underlying story, also related to the simulation hypothesis. The action (Gun-Fu) scenes are awesome. I could watch it over and over.
